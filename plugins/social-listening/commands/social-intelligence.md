# Social Intelligence (Trigify-Powered)

Your AI agent now has access to the entire social web. Monitor competitors, track your brand, analyze content gaps, and surface buying signals — all powered by Trigify's social listening MCP.

You have the following social intelligence skills available. Present them to the user in a clean table and ask what they'd like to work on:

| Skill | Command | What it does |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| Social Listening | `/social-listening` | Create searches, fetch posts, analyze results across X, Reddit, YouTube, LinkedIn, Podcasts. Monitor keywords, competitors, or your brand. |
| Competitive Intelligence | `/competitive-intel` | Monitor competitors across platforms. Track product launches, positioning, content strategy. Generate battle cards and competitive reports. |
| Brand Monitor | `/brand-monitor` | Track brand mentions, sentiment, and post performance. Get data-driven iteration suggestions. Monitor reputation across the social web. |
